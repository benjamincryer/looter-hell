shop: merchants pass by every x days, stay for y days

crafting templates: stages can drop a template with weapon type,level,body.
player can craft guns from these, materials dropped from stages

gems: grants bonus properties to embedded guns/shields

quest system: rewards for completing milestone stages

relics: gives special effect to player, like rings in an RPG

Can be allocated to: Player, Action Skill, Passive Skill

Unlock Suits instead of new characters (ie. warframes), can keep one player character throughout the story this way
